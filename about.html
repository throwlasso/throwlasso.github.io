--- title: Our Story description: >- I grew up in a household that clipped coupons from the newspaper. Budgets were tight and every dollar counted. My mom would clip them, sort them, and store them in a coupon folder for use at the next grocery store trip.

Over the years coupons have moved from newspapers to online. Now you click them to clip them.

I wrote this out of love for my mom who doesn't need to clip coupons anymore but still does because she loves a good deal

And for my fellow humans on this amazing planet who could better spend their money and time on things that make this world a better place. ---
